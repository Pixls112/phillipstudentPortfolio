About Me

Hey, I'm Phillip Nguyen. I'm passionate about playing games, especially things related to Souls-like or Dungeons and Dragons. I also love building PCs and customizing them to my own needs. When I'm not gaming or building PCs, I like to read manga or watch anime shows. I'm always looking for new ways to have fun and explore the world of gaming. No matter what I do, my goal is to make sure that everyone around me is having a good time.
